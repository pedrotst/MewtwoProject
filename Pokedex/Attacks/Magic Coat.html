Battle Effect:
A barrier reflects back to the target moves like Leech Seed and moves that damage status.
In-Depth Effect:

Magic Coat will reflect the moves used by the opponent on the Pokémon during the next turn back at them. This means that any moves which would cause stat reduction or status condition by the opponent onto you will then affect the user of those moves. The moves are as follows
